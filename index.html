About Us

Welcome to our company! We are a proudly youth-owned company that has been partnering with global ICT brands since 2018. Our business model is centered around establishing strong collaborations with leading companies in the Information and Communication Technology (ICT) industry.

At the helm of our organization is our founder, Fortune Mokoni. With a vision to create a dynamic and innovative company, Fortune established this venture to leverage the power of partnerships and propel our growth in the ICT sector.

Since our inception, we have been dedicated to building strategic alliances with renowned global ICT brands. These partnerships allow us to harness their expertise, technology, and resources, enabling us to deliver cutting-edge solutions and services to our valued clients
